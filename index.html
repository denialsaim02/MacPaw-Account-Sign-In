How Do I Sign In to My MacPaw Account?

MacPaw is a renowned software company that provides useful tools for macOS users, including CleanMyMac X, Setapp, and Gemini. Steps to Sign In to Your MacPaw Account

Follow these simple steps to sign in to your account:

  1. Go to the official sign-in page: https://macpawaccount.com
  2. Enter your registered email address in the login form.
  3. Type your password and click the “Sign In” button.
  4. Alternatively, sign in using your Google or Apple credentials for quicker access.

What If You Forget Your Password?

No worries — click on the “Forgot password?” link on the login page. You'll receive an email with password reset instructions. Make sure to check your spam or junk folder if you don’t see the email within a few minutes.

Troubleshooting Common Sign-In Issues

If you’re having trouble signing in, here are a few things to check:

  • Correct Credentials: Double-check your email and password for typos.
  • Browser Compatibility: Use a modern browser like Chrome, Safari, or Firefox.
  • Cookies & JavaScript: Ensure both are enabled in your browser settings.
  • Internet Connection: A slow or unstable connection may interfere with the process.
  • Clear Cache: Sometimes a simple cache clear can resolve login issues.

Account Security Tips

To keep your MacPaw account secure, follow these best practices:

  • Use a unique, strong password that includes numbers, symbols, and letters.
  • Do not share your login credentials with anyone.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) if available.
  • Log out of your account on shared or public computers.

Need Help? Contact Support
